VacuumSwitchTest.vi

 DecimalIn[DecimalIn]

a. Enter a value for “DecimalIn”. When you run the program, this number is converted to 30 bit binary and those bit combinations are used to control vacuum switches.

b. If the vacuum switches are working and no leak, you should get DecimalIn = DecimalExpected

c. "Switch status" shows the status of vacuums and gantry microswitch.

ControlType[ControlType]

ControlType:

1. DecimalSwitch:

Uses the i/p number from "DecimalIn" and controls the vacuum switches by converting decimal to 30 bit binary. Ignores Binary switches.

2. BinarySwitch:

Based on the switches shown in FP, vacuum switches are turned on/off. Ignores DecimalIn.

3. Conversion:

Reads the status of vacuum switches and shows them in "Switch status". Ignores DecimalIn and binary inputs.
